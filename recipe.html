Piccata

Click here for recipe page

Ingredients Directions
2-4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves, Pinch ground black pepper, 1/2 cup chicken stock or dry white wine Prepare chicken breats cut them in halves,Pound them with a meat hammer to 1/4-inch thickness
1/2 cup flour, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/4 cup brined capers, Pinch ground black pepper Mix the flour, salt, pepper, and grated parmesan together. Dredge the chicken breast through the flour mixture.Medium Heat olive oil and 2 tablespoons of the butter in skillet.Place the chicken in for 3 minutes each side
3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese, 4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil, 4 tablespoons butter, 2 tablespoons lemon juice, 2 tablespoons fresh chopped parsley Add the chicken stock, lemon juice, and capers to the pan. Whisk the remaining tablespoons of butter, and cook the sauce. place the chicken and pour the sauce over it.Sprinkle with parsley
